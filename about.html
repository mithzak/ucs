United Communication Services is a Private Limited Company, which is the leading Cable Television Distribution Company of Bangladesh. The company is a consortium of 24 MCR (Master Control Room) with more than 350 LCO (Local Cable Operator) catering to 0.9 million C&S Homes (Cable and Satellite Homes) in greater Dhaka entailing about 45-50% of the market share and reaching 70% of Dhaka city.

The company offers up to 90 Channels currently using the Analogue signal system. It also has a Digital Service with 110 Channels with 2000 STB (Set of box) deployed. It has the largest Cable Television Network in the City.

United Communication Services was incorporated on 22 November 2003 with a view to providing superior service to the viewers by introducing more number of satellite channels and ensuring better quality picture.

United Communication Service is run by a management team with more than 20 years of Cable & Satellite TV experience. As on June 2012, the company has more than 500 employees (direct & indirect) working for it. United Communication Service was the first MSO (Multi System Operator) to introduce Optical Fiber Network in Bangladesh.
